Statistical Modeling Final (R)

End-to-end modeling across three regimes: high-dimensional regression (lasso/ridge/elastic net), overdispersed count data (Poisson → NegBin), and imbalanced classification (logit → CART/RF/XGBoost).

Highlights

  • High-dimensional regression: handled p ≈ n by regularization and validated with residual/QQ/leverage diagnostics.
  • Count modeling: diagnosed severe overdispersion and selected Negative Binomial based on dispersion + deviance.
  • Classification: compared linear baseline to tree/ensemble models; prioritized stability and interpretability.
